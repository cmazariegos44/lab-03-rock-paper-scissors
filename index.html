Welcome to Rock-Paper-Scissors! I'm sure you're aware of the rules for this game but let's review just incase... You're going to need to choose between rock, paper, or scissor. Each one wins against the other in this fashion:
  • Rock beats Scissor
  • Scissor beats Paper
  • Paper beats rock
The computer is going to pick one at random and your goal is to win! Good luck!
